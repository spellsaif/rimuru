# Sovereign Circles

Circles bridge Rimuru to your personal messaging apps without sacrificing privacy. They act as "Sovereign Nodes" in your communication network.

## The WhatsApp Sovereign Node

The WhatsApp Circle is our flagship communication bridge. It allows you to carry your Sovereign AI in your pocket without exposing your data to the world.

### Strict Privacy Logic

Unlike other WhatsApp bots that listen to every group and contact, Rimuru is **Hard-Locked** to you.

1. **`isMe` Verification**: Rimuru uses the WhatsApp `isMe` property to ensure it ONLY replies to the account owner.
2. **Private "You" Chat**: It is designed to work in your own private thread (the chat where you can message yourself).
3. **Silent by Default**: It will never see, log, or reply to your friends, family, or work groups.
4. **Recursive Loop Prevention**: In self-chats, the bot is the "same" identity as the user. Rimuru uses advanced text-fingerprinting and ID-tracking to ensure it doesn't enter an infinite reply loop with itself.

### High-Speed Interaction

- **Direct Signal**: Real-time "Typing..." indicators are signaled directly to your phone while the AI is reasoning locally.
- **QR Pairing**: Access is granted via a one-time QR scan. Because you've already authenticated via QR, Rimuru bypasses secondary pairing codes for your own private chat.

## Technical Implementation

Circles are managed by the **Gate Server**. When a message arrives at the Gate:
1. The Gate Normalizes the message into a standard `Flow` event.
2. The Kernel processes the event and determines if a response is needed.
3. The response is formatted for the specific platform (e.g., converting Markdown to WhatsApp syntax) and sent back through the Gate.

### Supported Adapters
- **WhatsApp**: Fully hardened and sovereign-ready.
- **Slack**: (Experimental) For workspace automation.
- **Telegram**: (In Development) Focusing on encrypted chat support.
